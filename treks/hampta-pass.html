Hampta Pass

The best winter trek in the Himalayas

Trek Itinerary

Day 1 : Drive from Manali to Indiahikes Jungle Line Campus, drive to Jobra Roadhead, and trek to Jobra Campsite
Drive distance: 50 km | Drive duration: 2-3 hrTrek distance: 1.5 km | Trek duration: 1 hours
Start your adventure with a scenic drive from Manali to Jobra Roadhead, followed by a short trek to Jobra Campsite. Enjoy the picturesque views of the surrounding mountains.
Day 2 : Trek from Jobra to Jwara
Trek distance: 4.5 km | Trek duration: 4 hours
Day 3 : Trek from Jwara to Balu Ka Ghera
Trek distance: 5 km | Trek duration: 4 hours
Day 4 : Trek from Balu ka Ghera to Shea Goru via Hampta Pass
Trek distance: 8 km | Trek duration: 9 hours
Day 5 : Trek from Shea Goru to Chhatru.
Trek distance: 7 km | Trek duration: 4-5 hours
Day 6 : Drive from Chhatru to Manali via Chandratal
Drive distance(including Chandratal): 160 km | Drive duration: 9-10 hr
