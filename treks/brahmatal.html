Brahmatal Trek

The best winter trek in the Himalayas

Trek Itinerary

Day 1 : Drive from Rishikesh to Lohajung
Drive distance: 270 km | Drive duration: 10-11 hr
Start your adventure with a scenic drive from Rishikesh to Lohajung, the base camp for the Brahmatal trek. Enjoy the picturesque views along the way.
Day 2 : Trek from Lohajung to Gujreni
Trek distance: 4.5 km | Trek duration: 5 hours
Day 3 : Trek from Gujreni to Tilandi
Trek distance: 2.9 km | Trek duration: 4-5 hours
Day 4 : Trek from Tilandi to Brahmatal via Brahmatal Top
Trek distance: 6 km | Trek duration: 5-6 hours
Day 5 : Trek from Brahmatal to Lohajung
Trek distance: 8.45 km | Trek duration: 7-8 hours
Day 6 : Drive from Lohajung to Rishikesh
Drive distance: 270 km | Drive duration: 10-11 hr
